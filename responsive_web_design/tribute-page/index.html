Dr. Esther Edward

The Woman who nurtured the symbol of Teleportation and FTL Travel

Here is a time line of Dr. Esther's life:

  • 1970 - Born in Jos, Plateu Nigeria
  • 1978 - Her and family relocated to Payi Billiri Gombe Bauchi State, Plunged into Poverty and started the struggle to survive as a child
  • 1979 - Has to trek average of 30km a day to attend school."
  • 1989 - Finishes senior secondary school bajoga
  • 1990 - Marries pastor Edward Obeth Dangombe
  • 1991 - Give birth to her first Son
  • 1993 - Finishes Junior Community Health College JCHEW at Health technological college Kagoro jos and takes a voluntary job at ECWA nine staff school clinic
